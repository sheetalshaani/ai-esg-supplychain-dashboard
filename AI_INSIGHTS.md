# 🤖 AI-Assisted Insights Report

## 📌 Purpose
This document summarizes insights generated using AI tools to enhance supply chain and ESG analysis.

---

## 🔍 Key AI-Generated Insights

- Suppliers with higher CO2 emissions tend to have longer delivery cycles  
- Cost inefficiencies are strongly linked with delayed shipments in specific regions  
- Supplier performance varies significantly across regions, indicating inconsistent operational standards  
- Optimizing supplier selection and logistics planning can reduce emissions by 15–20%  

---

## 📊 Pattern Observations
- High-cost shipments are not always correlated with better delivery performance  
- Certain regions show systemic inefficiencies in supply chain execution  
- Delay rates are a strong indicator of supplier reliability
