# 📊 Business Case: AI in ESG & Supply Chain Analytics

---

## 🧩 Problem Statement
Organizations struggle to connect ESG metrics with operational supply chain performance, leading to fragmented decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To demonstrate how structured KPIs and AI-assisted analysis can improve visibility into supply chain efficiency and sustainability performance.

---

## 🔍 Approach
- Defined ESG and supply chain KPIs  
- Mapped relationships between cost, emissions, and delivery performance  
- Used AI to simulate insights and identify patterns  
- Built a conceptual analytics framework  

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights
- Sustainability and cost efficiency often have trade-offs  
- Supplier performance directly impacts ESG outcomes  
- Data-driven supplier evaluation improves overall efficiency  

---

## 🚀 Business Impact
If implemented in real enterprise systems, this framework can:
- Improve ESG compliance tracking  
- Reduce operational inefficiencies  
- Enable data-driven supplier decisions  
- Enhance sustainability reporting accuracy  

---

## 💡 AI Contribution
AI assisted in:
- Structuring business logic  
- Identifying KPI relationships  
- Simulating enterprise insights
